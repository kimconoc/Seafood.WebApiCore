> Tìm hiểu về ICommand, Command in button wpf

> Di chuyển window khi press vào control bar : Install-Package System.Windows.Interactivity.WPF
xaml, let this into <Window></Window>: <i:Interaction.Triggers>
        <i:EventTrigger EventName="SelectionChanged">
            <i:InvokeCommandAction Command="{Binding SelectedItemChangedCommand}"/>
        </i:EventTrigger>
    </i:Interaction.Triggers>
